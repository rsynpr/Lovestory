Our Story

A little story about us

We both went to school together, maybe we weren't very close at the time, but there was a bond formed between us.

Over the years, we lived our own lives. Perhaps we experienced joy, sadness, and other life experiences. One day, fate brought us to meet despite starting from an Instagram DM and this is where conversations started, memories were shared, and a strong connection was felt.

Then, from the new friendship, love grew. We started to realize that we might have had deeper feelings for each other all along. And finally, we decided to give our relationship a chance. With every day we spent together, our love grew and strengthened.

Despite the challenges we have faced along the way, including misunderstandings and distance, our love has only grown stronger. We stand side by side in joy and sorrow, weathering the storms of life together. In the end, Rasya and Nasywa's love story is proof of the power of connection, perseverance, and the belief that true love knows no boundaries.
